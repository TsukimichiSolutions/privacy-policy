Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

Cozy Pocket ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled in our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • We do not use analytics tools.
  • We do not request or store any personal identifiers (e.g., name, email, phone).
  • All your data (like incomes, expenses, notes) is saved locally on your device and not transmitted elsewhere.

2. Local Data Storage

All financial and personal information entered in the app is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any dangerous permissions (such as access to your contacts, camera, or location).

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services that collect information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Updates will be posted here and in the app.
